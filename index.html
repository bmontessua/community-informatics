Empower with Technology Initiative

Lets bring equity to computer and broadband access.

We want to bring computing equity to our small towns across North Carolina that may not have access to a library. The solution we are proposing is simple: lets get our faith based institutions and other interested businesses the resources to bring public computing to their locations!

There is no cost associated, we are here to provide our expertice and resources to facilitate the implementation of public computer access or public Wi-Fi! If this sounds interesting to you, please use our form to contact us or you can also go learn more about us here.
